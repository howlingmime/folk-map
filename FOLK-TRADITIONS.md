# Folk Music Traditions of the World

A comprehensive guide to the folk music traditions documented in this application.

## Overview

This application covers **approximately 90 folk music traditions** across **13 geographic regions**, representing musical heritage from every inhabited continent. Each tradition includes notable artists, key recordings, historical context, and subgenres.

## New Fields in Data

Each tradition now includes:
- **notableArtists**: Array of important artists in the tradition
- **keyRecordings**: Essential albums and compilations
- **historicalContext**: Historical background and development
- **subgenres**: Related styles and regional variations

## Data Source

All folk tradition data is defined in `src/data/folkMusic.js` and displayed throughout the application, particularly on the World Music page.
